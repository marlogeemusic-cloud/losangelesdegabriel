Our story · The why behind it all

Marlogee & Gabriel

A foundation born from a mother's love — and from a promise that no one else should suffer alone.

In loving memory

Gabriel Ángel Maciel

Sunrise · October 26, 1992 🕊️

In December 2014, this foundation was created in memory of my son, Gabriel Ángel Maciel. When Gabriel passed away, I did not have the resources for his funeral.

I asked the city for help, and I was passed from one person to another — six different people — having to repeat my painful story over and over, only to be told in the end that they could not help me. It was frustrating and heartbreaking.

“I decided that no one else should ever go through that.”

“Baby boy… like you used to tell me — ‘it's all good mommy, I love you patches.’ I love you too, my patches, and always will.”

The founder

Marlogee

Gabriel's mother · Houston, Texas

Out of her own loss, Marlogee turned grief into a mission. Since 2014 she has cooked and sold plates of food to raise funds, helping families with medical bills, funeral costs and everyday needs.

In our foundation we don't ask bureaucratic questions. If someone needs help and there are no direct funds, we organize a food benefit immediately — always asking a relative to collect the money directly, for full transparency.

🎶

Beyond the foundation, Marlogee also loves to sing — finding light and healing in music.

The food-benefit model

Community fed, families lifted
